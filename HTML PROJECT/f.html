BEST DEVOTINALTRIPS




KEDARNATH

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, Kedarnath Temple is among the 275 temples of Paadal Petra Sthalams (the most powerful Shiva temples in the world) and is also the most important among the Panch Kedars.

BADRINATH

Badrinath or Badrinarayana Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu. It is situated in the town of Badrinath in Uttarakhand, India.The temple is mentioned in ancient religious texts like Vishnu Purana and Skanda Purana.

TIRUPATI

Tirupati is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is the administrative headquarters of the Tirupati district. Tirupati is known as the abode of the Hindu god Venkateshvara, Lord of Seven Hills.
