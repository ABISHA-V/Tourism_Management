KEDARNATH

A spiritual holiday in the sacred city ofKedarnath is a good way to relieve yourself from the modern fast-paced life. Visit the peaceful temples and mosques here and enjoy some quiet time with in the tranquil environment of these religious sites. For a hassle-free trip in Kedarnath, people can have a heart-warming experience. While you are on spiritual tour here, you will come across thousands of devotees who visit Kedarnath all round the year to offer prayers to sacred deities. You can also find numerous smaller shrines in every corner of Kedarnath that are built by the locals. A walk through the narrow streets of Kedarnath can be a wonderful experience
